How to Use Hablas

What is Hablas?

Hablas helps you learn how to speak a language through:

  • Reading books in your target language
  • Listening to proper pronunciation
  • Practicing speaking out loud

1. Select Your Book

Start by choosing a book using either:

  • The "Tap here to begin!" button, or
  • The "Available books" option in settings

2. Choose a Sentence

Select any sentence you want to practice reading aloud.

3. Record Your Voice

Tap the record button and read the sentence out loud.

4. Stop Recording

After reading the sentence, tap the stop button.

5. Check Your Pronunciation

Review your recording and tap any word to hear its correct pronunciation.
